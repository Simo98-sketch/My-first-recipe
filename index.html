POLENTA BRESCIANA - GIALLO ZAFFERANO

 

For each lt of water 210 g of flour, 12 g of salt and one spoon of oil. Add salt and oil, salt dissolved, add flour and mix all. Cook for 60 minutes and mix.  

  • Difficulty: medium
  • Preparation: 20 m
  • Cooking: 60 m
  • Doses for: 4 people
  • Cost: very low
    •  
      210 g of flour
      12 g of salt
      One spoon of oil
 

Buon appetito!

Go to polenta!  

CHICKEN WITH ALMONDS - GIALLO ZAFFERANO

 

Cook the chicken, add some soy sauce and a spoon of almonds. Then mix all and serve it!  

  • Difficulty: easy
  • Preparation: 20 m
  • Cooking: 30 m
  • Doses for: 2 people
  • Cost: low
    •  
      Chicken
      Almonds
      Bamboo shoots
      Soy sauce
 

 

Buon appetito!

  Go to chicken and almonds!
