POLENTA BRESCIANA - GIALLO ZAFFERANO

For each lt of water 210 g of flour, 12 g of salt and one spoon of oil. Add salt and oil, salt dissolved, add flour and mix all. Cook for 60 minutes and mix.  
  • Difficulty: medium
  • Preparation: 20 m
  • Cooking: 60 m
  • Doses for: 4 people
  • Cost: very low
    •  
  • 210 g of flour
  • 12 g of salt
  • One spoon of oil
 

 

Buon appetito!

Go to polenta!

- SPAGHETTI ALLA CARBONARA - GIALLO ZAFFERANO

 

Cook spaghetti for 15 minutes, meanwhile mix egg and goat cheese. Cook pillow and then add it to spaghetti. When spaghetti are ready, add eggs and cheese. Mix all and serve it!  

  • Difficulty: easy
  • Preparation: 15 m
  • Cooking: 10 m
  • Doses for: 4 people
  • Cost: low
    •  
  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pillow
  • Goat cheese
 

 

Buon appetito!

  Go to spaghetti!
